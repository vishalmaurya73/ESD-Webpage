ABOUT DISTRIC

An ESD program is a comprehensive set of measures designed to prevent, control, and manage electrostatic discharge (ESD) in environments where sensitive electronic components are present. The program aims to protect these components from damage caused by static electricity, which can lead to reliability issues and premature failures. Key components of an ESD program include employee training on ESD risks and prevention techniques, the use of ESD control products like mats, wrist straps, and footwear, and the establishment of ESD Protected Areas (EPAs) with properly grounded workstations.
